About Me

i play videogames



Year 1 Season 1

I didn't play the beta, but I got the game when it released and I've been playing it since then. I was always at a pretty high rank, but I wasn't into the esports scene yet. One day when I was playing ranked, I was topfragging while we destroyed the other team. It turns out, the enemy team was Empire Esports. After the game, the leader added me, messaged me and told me they want me to join their team.


Year 1 Season 2

During Year 1 Season 2, I joined the roster for E2 and played in the pro league for Year 1 Season 2. We didn't complete the season because of internal issues, followed by a disbandment. During this season is when I created my YouTube channel. At the time, my future in the Esports scene was uncertain.


Year 1 Season 3

During Year 1 Season 3, I joined Lunatik Esports as a sub. They ended up finishing third in the pro league season.


Year 1 Season 4

When Season 4 started, I was subbing for Ferocity Esports (onslaught). Halfway through the season, I left because I didn't want to be a sub and I was still 17 at the time. I didn't want to jeopardize their pro league spot if I was caught for being underage. Ferocity ended up finishing fourth in that season.
I wanted to leave and create my own team instead, so I formed the team for Golden R6 and we played in the pro league qualifiers for the following season. We won the tournament, beating Most Wanted (eRa) in the finals. This gave us a spot in the pro league. However, they had suspicions that Golden R6 had an underage player. The qualifiers were a week before I turned 18, and I was investigated by ESL admins. The team was banned, so we disbanded.


Year 2 Season 1

In Year 2 I turned 18 and formed Everlasting Esports with 3 of the former Golden R6 members, and we played in the qualifiers for the Challenger league. We won the Challenger league, beating Soar (Reckless). The following day, one of my teammates was banned from an ESL admin investigation. His ESL name didn't match his UPlay account name, and the whole team was disqualified after we won.


Year 1 Season 2, 3, and 4

During these seasons I spent time working on my YouTube channel and focusing on school. I've recently been playing on a variety of different Challenger league teams in order to keep up with the competitive scene.
